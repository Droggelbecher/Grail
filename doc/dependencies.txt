
Development versions of the following are required for compiling:

* libboost, boost-filesystem, boost-system, boost-regex
  (tested with boost version 1.46)
* libsdl, libsdl-ttf, libsdl-image, libsdl-gfx, libsdl-mixer
* liblua>=5.1
* libluabind >=0.8.1
* libpng
* alure
  source: http://www.kcat.strangesoft.net/alure.html
